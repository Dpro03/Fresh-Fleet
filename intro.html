Introducing our website!

and how to use it....

Let's get started! Take a look around our website. If you want to place an order or just want to browse our products, you can do that by going to our home page and click on the products link at the top of the page. When you get to our products pages, you will see links on the sides of the pages where you will be able to see other categories of products. If you do not see a link on one side of a page, that means you have come to the end of the categories. If a link is not working, please
contact us.
You can find our contact information on our home page or by clicking the contact us button. By the way, every page has a 'home' button at the top of the page that will take you back to the home page.
